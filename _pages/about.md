I am with the **GLM team at [Zhipu AI](https://www.zhipuai.cn/)**, where I work on **SFT data infrastructure** and **agentic capabilities** — currently focused on **Office Agent** (improving the *Agent Mode* on [Z.ai](https://z.ai)) and **[GDPval](https://openai.com/index/gdpval/)** (strengthening the model's ability to solve real-world economic tasks). I serve as a **core contributor** to [*GLM-5*](#glm-5), and also contributed to [*GLM-4.5*](#glm-4-5).

Before joining Zhipu, I received my Master's degree in Electronic Information from **Peking University** (2022–2025), advised by Prof. [Yanbing Jiang](https://www.ss.pku.edu.cn/teacherteam/teacherlist/1613-%E8%92%8B%E4%B8%A5%E5%86%B0.html). During graduate school I interned at **ByteDance**, **Ant Group**, **HKUST**, and **Yuanfudao**, with work spanning LLM training & post-training, computer vision, privacy-preserving ML, and large-scale backend systems.
